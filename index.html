Types of Mike and Ikes

There are dozens of flavors, even some that are only seasonal for holiday seasons. There are 12 core flavors:

  • Original Fruits
  • Tropical Typhoon
  • Berry Blast
  • Mega Mix
  • Mega Mix Sour
  • Mega Mix Sour
  • Sour-Licious Original
  • Sour-Licious Zours Intense Fruit
  • Red Rageous
  • Jolly Joes
  • Cotton Candy
  • Cherry
  • Strawberry Reunion

History of Mike and Ike

Mike and Ikes were first introduced in 1940 by the Just Born, Inc. candy company. The company was founded by Sam Born. Over the decades, Mike and Ike has released many flavors and variations. Mike and Ikes remain a nostalgic favorite and continute to be widely sold in theaters, stores, and vending machines across the U.S.
